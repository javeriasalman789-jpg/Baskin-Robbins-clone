FAQ

How do I create a Baskin-Robbins Rewards account?

Download the BR App and head to the Sign Up/ Log In page by tapping on
the pink spoons icon.Click Sign Up at the botton of the page, add your
info,and don't forget to check the box to join Baskin-Robbins Rewards
before completing sign up!

I already have a Baskin-Robbins account, do I need to create a new
one to join Baskin-Robbins Rewards?

Nope! Simply sign into your existing account on the BR App,click into the
Rewards button in the bottom navigation bar and click "Join". Piece of
(ice cream) cake.

How can I access my Baskin-Robbins Rewards in the BR App?

You can browse available rewards and offers by logging into your Baskin-
Robbins account on the BR App and clicking on the Rewards button in the
bottom navigation bar.Not signed up yet? No problem! Tap the "Join
Baskin-Robbins Rewards" button and get the (ice cream) party started.

How do I earn rewards/offer?

Every visit to Baskin-Robbins brings you closer to your next reward! To
earn a visit credit on an in-shop transaction,ensure to present your
Baskin-Robbins Rewards QR code to be scanned at checkout.For BR App
or BaskinRobbins.com orders,make sure you are signed into your Baskin-
Robbins Rewards account before checking out.

How can I redeem my rewards/offers?

Rewards and offers can be redeemed in the BR App, either in-shop by applying the rewards and/or offers to your Baskin-Robbins Rewards QR code before scanning at the register or on BR App orders by applying the rewards and/or offers to your cart before placing your order. Additionally, as long as you are logged in to your Rewards account, Baskin-Robbins Rewards members can get credit for visits & use rewards/offers for online & BR App orders.

Which purchases do Baskin-Robbins Rewards members earn visit credits for?

Baskin-Robbins Rewards members earn a visit credit for every transaction with a total of $1+ at participating locations, except for the following: the purchase or reload of Baskin-Robbins Gift Cards, bottled beverages, reward redemptions, retail merchandise, donations to charity, products purchased on shopbaskinrobbins.com and other online platforms, and Dunkin’® products sold in Dunkin'/Baskin-Robbins combo locations. Please note that Baskin-Robbins Rewards members can only earn 1 visit credit every 10 minutes. See Baskin-Robbins Rewards program terms and conditions for full details.

Can I earn visit credits and/or redeem rewards or offers on Delivery transactions?

No, Delivery orders are not eligible to earn visit credits or to redeem rewards or offers at this time

I ordered on the BR App/BaskinRobbins.com, but I’m not seeing my visit credit – what happened?

Visit credits for orders placed on the BR App and BaskinRobbins.com will be issued at the time of order pickup.

Do my rewards and offers expire?

Yes. To check the expiration date of your rewards/offers, head to the BR App – expiration dates are listed on each reward and offer.

Can I use more than 1 reward or offer in a single transaction?

Yes! You can redeem a combination of up to 3 rewards and offers per transaction. Please note there is a limit of one offer applied per transaction, and always be sure to read each reward or offer’s description and terms for more information on redeeming.

How do I get a free welcome scoop?

Your free welcome scoop will be added to your Baskin-Robbins Rewards account after your first qualified purchase. See Terms Of Use for qualified purchases.
